Talk of current day Telugu film industry and the conversation cannot be complete without the mention of Vijay Sai Devarakonda, the highly-talented, versatile superstar of Tollywood. Born to an actor father, it was natural for young Vijay to make a choice. Like his father, he found his love in front of camera and soon started following the profession passionately. While belonging to a family of actor did give Devarakonda that extra edge, no one can deny his inherent flair and skill for it. After making his foray in theatre, Devarakonda soon opted for big screen. After his minor role debut, Devarakonda’s big breakthrough came with ‘Yevade Subramanyam’. However, it was ‘Pelli Choopulu’ that marked the beginning of the golden period for this ace actor. Since the 2015 blockbuster, there has been no stopping Devarakonda who has belted out one box office hit after the other. He is the heartthrob of Telugu film industry and has everything going for him—good looks, great physique, attractive personality, an a rising career and bucket.
